# 48-Hour Verified Audit — Product Template
*Fixed-scope, client-ready. Variants: website / Etsy listing / Google Business Profile / email flow.*

## The offer (copy-paste)
**"48-Hour [Website|Etsy|GBP] Audit — verified findings, ready-to-ship fixes. $150 fixed."**
You get: a prioritized top-10 fix list, 3 rewritten titles, meta/description rewrite, H2/section outline, 5 FAQs, and a full rewrite of your weakest section. Every finding is verified against your live listing — no AI guesswork. Delivered as PDF + 5-min Loom in 48 hours.

Upsell path: audit ($150) -> implementation ($250-400) -> monthly retainer ($300-500/mo).

## Packet structure (fill per client)
1. **Cover**: client name, date, scope, one-line diagnosis.
2. **Search intent**: what buyers actually type; where the client is invisible.
3. **Top-10 fixes**, priority-ordered, each with: the flaw (screenshot/citation), why it costs money, the exact fix.
4. **Rewrites**: 3 title options, meta description, H2 outline, 5 FAQs, weakest-section rewrite.
5. **Verification appendix**: evidence for every claim (lie-detector stamp).
6. **Next step**: fixed-price implementation offer.
