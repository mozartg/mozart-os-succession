# Quick-Fix Menu — the offer buyers actually want (supersedes audit-as-product)
*Pivot 2026-07-07, based on buyer sentiment: nobody buys reports. They buy fixes, installed, fast. "Freezer to microwave" — done when it arrives.*

## Rule
The audit is INTERNAL ONLY: it's how we find the flaw and pick the fix. The buyer never sees the word "audit." They see a before/after.

- **$99 — 24-hr Listing Rescue (Etsy/GBP):** I rewrite and republish your title, description, tags, and FAQs. Live before you wake up. Before/after screenshot included.

## Positioning line
"No reports. No calls. You point at the problem, it's fixed and live in 48 hours — with a before/after so you can see it."

## Outreach (the free sample IS a fix)
Cold-DM: "Hi [name] — your [site/listing] has [specific verified flaw]. I fixed the first one free — here's the before/after mock. Want the other [n] fixed and live by Friday? $[menu price], flat."
The leave-behind is a screenshot of the fix already mocked up, not a findings PDF. The lie-detector gate still applies: nothing claimed 'fixed' without the live URL / run proof.

## Ladder
Free mocked fix -> $99-249 menu item -> $300-500/mo "handyman retainer": 4 fixes a month, 48-hr SLA, no scoping calls.
