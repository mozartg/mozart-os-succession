# Offer Straight-Shooter

Trigger: before building, listing, or pitching any new offer.

## Method
1. State the offer in one sentence: who pays, what they get, price, turnaround.
2. Answer bluntly: Who is the buyer, and where are 10 of them findable this week?
3. Name the 3 nearest competitors and why this beats them (or admit it doesn't).
4. Effort-to-first-dollar estimate in hours. If >20 hrs to first plausible dollar, flag it.
5. Verdict: GO / GO-IF (one condition) / KILL. No hedging.

## Quality checklist
- [ ] Buyer location named specifically (a subreddit, a search term, a job feed)
- [ ] A real competitor cited, not "no one is doing this"
- [ ] Verdict is one of the three, stated first

## Rerunnable prompt
"Act as the offer straight-shooter. Offer: [describe]. Give the verdict first, then the one-sentence offer, buyer location, competitor reality, and hours-to-first-dollar."
