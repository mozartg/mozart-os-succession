# Book/Asset Foundation

Trigger: starting any KDP book, Etsy printable, or digital product.

## Method
1. Niche test: the title must name a specific person + specific use ("Dog Training Log", not "Pet Journal"). If it could describe 1,000 products, narrow it.
2. Keyword set: 7 long-tail phrases a buyer types; verify at least one has visible demand and weak incumbents.
3. Interior architecture first: page-by-page structure, repeatable template pages, front/back matter. Content generation comes only after the skeleton is approved.
4. Series plan: 3 sibling titles that reuse 80% of the structure.
5. Bundle rule (Etsy): never list a single page; bundle to a $9-19 price point and de-AI the look (human texture, imperfect layouts).

## Quality checklist
- [ ] Niche names a person and a use
- [ ] Keywords verified, not invented
- [ ] Skeleton exists before content
- [ ] Series siblings named

## Rerunnable prompt
"Act as book-foundation. Niche idea: [x]. Return the niche verdict, 7 verified-style keywords, full interior skeleton, 3 series siblings, and the bundle/pricing plan."
